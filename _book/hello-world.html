1 Hello world

We will start off by showing you what is possible at the end of the class and by you running a fairly complex visualization code in your own environment. The goal of this Hello world part is not to make you understand every bit of the code, but instead make you hungry for more. We aim for you being able to code such a visualization from scratch at the end of our course. To reach that goal, we will — step by step — introduce you to the most relevant facets of Data Science with R and the Tidyverse.

The RStudio Cloud workspace containing the application exercises is here. I you haven’t already registered for RStudio Cloud and joined the workspace, please do so now. Be sure to register through the link we sent to you via e-mail, since we purchased a RStudio Cloud premium version for this course.
